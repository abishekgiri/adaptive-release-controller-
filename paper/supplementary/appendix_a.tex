\section{Appendix A: Validity
Classification}\label{appendix-a-validity-classification}

\textbf{Labels:} - \textbf{Demonstrated} --- mechanism verified; result
is robust across n=30 seeds, multiple configurations, and/or follows
directly from code/math. Unlikely to reverse with more data. -
\textbf{Suggestive} --- result is real on the data collected, but scope
is limited (synthetic, 2 projects, short trajectory). Directionally
plausible; magnitude may shift. - \textbf{Speculative} --- claim
extrapolates beyond the experimental evidence, relies on analogy, or is
a conditional prediction not yet tested.
